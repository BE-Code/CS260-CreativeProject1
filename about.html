About Us

What is Barber Shop 260?

Barber Shop 260 is a place that is guaranteed to offer you the highest degree of luxury, quality, and comfort as you enjoy one of our premium haircuts or shaves. We pride ourselves in being one of the best in the business.

Why did you make Barber Shop 260

Why did we make it you ask? Our founder once went to schedule a hair cut at a local hair salon. On the way he discovered that their website needed help desperately. Then, in his web design class, CS 260, Dr. Clement inspired him to something greater. And thus, Barber Shop 260 was born.
